About Us

Welcome to our Todo App built with Angular! This app helps you manage your tasks and stay organized. With our user-friendly interface, you can easily add, delete, and update your todos. Whether it's for personal or professional use, our Todo App simplifies your task management process.

Key Features:

  • Add new todos with titles and descriptions
  • Mark todos as done or not done
  • Delete todos you no longer need
  • Edit existing todos to update their titles or descriptions
  • Automatic data persistence using local storage

Start organizing your tasks today and experience the convenience of our Todo App powered by Angular. Stay productive and make the most out of your day!
